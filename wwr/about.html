Ecuador Rafting

Our mission is to bring unforgettable whitewater rafting experiences in Ecuador with safety, fun, and nature at the heart of everything we do!

History

Founded in 2013, Ecuador Rafting started with one goal: to share the beauty of Ecuador's rivers. With over a decade of experience, we pride ourselves on safety, fun, and conservation.

Over the years, Ecuador Rafting has become a leader in eco-tourism and adventure sports in the region. From humble beginnings with a single raft, the company has grown to guide thousands of adventurers each year through some of South America's most breathtaking rivers, always promoting respect for nature and local communities.
